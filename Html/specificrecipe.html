Homemade Spaghetti Bolognese

Posted on June 14, 2025 by Chef Minh

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 200g minced beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil and saut√© onion and garlic.
  3. Add minced beef, cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomatoes, simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Combine with spaghetti and serve hot.

Tips

For extra flavor, add a splash of red wine while simmering the sauce. Garnish with fresh basil and parmesan cheese.
